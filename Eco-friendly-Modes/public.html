    Tram

    Trams are a clean, efficient, and reliable way to get around cities. They run on electricity, so they donâ€™t pollute the air and are much quieter than cars or buses. Trams help reduce traffic and support greener city living by encouraging people to use public transport instead of driving. They can carry many passengers at once and often run on their own tracks, which helps them avoid traffic and stay on schedule. Trams are also easy to use, with low floors for easy boarding and clear routes. Compared to trains, they are often cheaper to build and run, making them a smart and affordable option for cities and commuters alike.
